This Is My Bio Information

Salim Noori is a detail-oriented and results-driven Project Coordinator with a robust background in successfully leading complex projects across various sectors. With expertise in project management, contract negotiation, and budget control, Salim is dedicated to ensuring the efficient execution of organizational programs while adhering to timelines and budgetary constraints. In his current role as Program Coordinator at the Department of State, Salim oversees a $1.2 million training project for Civil and Foreign Service employees, demonstrating his ability to manage budgets, coordinate with stakeholders, and ensure program effectiveness. He has also managed multiple mega projects with the U.S. Agency for International Development (USAID), consistently delivering ahead of schedule and under budget, showcasing exceptional project management skills. Salim's proficiency in cross-functional collaboration, regulatory compliance, and customer relationship management has been instrumental in his career. His track record includes managing projects in diverse environments, from construction projects in Afghanistan to software development initiatives in collaboration with international vendors. Salim holds a Project Management Professional (PMP) certification along with certifications in financial management and human resources. With strong analytical abilities, excellent interpersonal skills, and a commitment to project success.
